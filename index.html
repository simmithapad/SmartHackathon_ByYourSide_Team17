About By Your Side


By Your Side is a Indian website that offers families, plaque suppliers, and funeral homes services for QR code and eTribute website pages. You may be confident that the life narrative of your loved ones will be preserved for future generations thanks to our QR Codes and eTribute website pages, which will survive for a very long time.

We also employ flexible dynamic QR Codes for you. Consequently, you won't need to replace the QR Code engraved on your loved ones' headstone or plaque in the future if you want to modify the website page that is linked to it.
